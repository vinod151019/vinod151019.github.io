Who I am

A technologist who thinks like a product owner

I'm an AWS Solutions Architect whose day job looks a lot like product management: I run discovery with founders, CTOs, and engineering teams; translate ambiguous business problems into technical roadmaps; make build-vs-buy and prioritization calls; and drive things from prototype to launch. My focus area is AI/ML, Generative AI, and Agentic AI — the fastest-moving product surface in tech.

Now I'm channeling that into a Technical Product Management path — where the technical depth, the customer obsession, and the commercial instinct all point in the same direction: shipping products people use.
